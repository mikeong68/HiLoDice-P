Hi-Lo Dice

You are the Player.
You be playing with the computer.
Both starts with 20 Hit Points.
First one to reach 0 Hit Point LOSE.

One dice has a value of between 1 to 6.
The seed with the highest value wins the round.
Winner increase Hit Points with total value of Dice(s).
Loser lose Hit Points with total value of Dice(s).

Only player has Shield Points starting from 5 in Game Mode #2 and #3. Shield Points are insurance against Computer Dice having higher total values than 6; cause Computer has two dice(s). When Shield is activated, the increase / losing of Hit Points will just be the highest total value of the two players - instead of the total.

i.e. Player = 5; Computer = 14 thus total value = 19
Shield ON - only 14 is used to adjust Hit Points while
Shield OFF - 19 is used to adjust Hit Points.
